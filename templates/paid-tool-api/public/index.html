paid-tool-api metered

One endpoint that summarizes text — and bills the caller's SettleMesh wallet per call.

Try it



  
Call it yourself

  
Sign in once at /__settle/login, then POST to the endpoint. The session cookie is your
     payer; the call is billed to your SettleMesh wallet in Aev (1 USD = 100 Aev).

  
curl -X POST https://YOUR-APP.run.settlemesh.io/api/tool \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer <your-settlemesh-session-token>" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"text":"Long article here...","style":"bullets"}'
